About The Company

What IS Take and Trade?

Do you have things laying around your house that you don’t use anymore? Do these things still have use? Would you like to make money off of your items without selling them? You can repurpose and lend out your things using Take and Trade!

Take and Trade is a social networking and online marketplace that allows members to share and borrow items with fellow neighbors. Take and Trade users can list their items, freely adjust daily borrow fees and time limits, and engage in lending with other users. Users can also leave reviews and message others directly, ensuring open and honest communication with the Take and Trade community as a whole, so you’ll never feel unsure of your lendings or borrows.

Why should YOU use Take and Trade?

Why let something sit in a storage room or a garage collecting dust when you can make money off of it? Take and Trade is a simple, easy way to monetize your belongings without getting rid of them. Not only is Take and Trade a great way to earn money passively for yourself, but it’s also great for your community, allowing others to have access to things that may not have been accessible by normal means.

OUR Mission

Our mission here at Take and Trade is to help people become more connected with their neighborhoods and keep the world a little greener. In past generations, not everybody owned a tent, a specialty tool or a bicycle. These things were commonly lent to trusted neighbors as a regular practice. Take and Trade is hoping to revive this tradition in a modern and digital way, making things more accessible to others. Using Take and Trade will also negate the need for throwing away unwanted items somewhere to rot and encourage recycling, sharing and community engagement.
